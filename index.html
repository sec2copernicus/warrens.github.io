Tentang Pantai Mangrove" Text Tentang Pantai Mangrove 1.The Importance of Mangrove Forests for the Environment The mangrove forest is a collection of trees that grow in the surrounding area the coastline which is affected by the ebb and flow of sea water and is at places that experience accumulation of organic matter and sludge. Forest Mangroves are also commonly known as mangrove forests an ecosystem that is unique because of the activity of the inundation cycle by the tides. In this habitat only mangrove / mangrove trees are able to survive due to the process of evolution and adaptation that has been bypassed by mangroves.Mangrove forests have a very large function for our environment among them namely 1) as a plant that is able to withstand the currents of sea water erode the coastal plains, in other words mangrove plants are able to to hold sea water so as not to erode the soil on the shoreline. 2) As function other plants, mangroves also have a function as a gas absorber carbon dioxide (CO2) and produces oxygen (O2). 3) Mangrove forests have role as a place to live for various kinds of marine biota such as small fish to take shelter and find food.
